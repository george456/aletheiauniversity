About Aletheia

Aletheia University, Ago-Iwoye is a state-of-the-art private University committed to providing world class education alongside contemporary skills for its students. Located along Obafemi Awolowo Way, Ago-Iwoye, Ogun State, the university creates an ideal learning environment for producing graduates with a knack for excellence and creativity.

Our Vision

The vision of Aletheia University, Ago-Iwoye, is “to be a world-class University with an ideal learning environment in which graduates with an entrepreneurial spirit, professional skills and creative minds with right human attitudes are produced.”

In line with the vision of the university, the conduct of its activities shall be ethical, truthful, and godly. These qualities, if inculcated into the daily lives of the youth, will help in building a better Nigeria. The programs being proposed are those involved in the application of technology in nature. These are at the forefront of global research and development, especially in the area of computer and internet technologies that the courses will be built around.

Our Mission

The mission of the Aletheia University, Ago-Iwoye is “to create and nurture the Nigerian of the future in ideals of the modern world truthfully and with the power to maintain the truth and act truthfully with all humanity”.
